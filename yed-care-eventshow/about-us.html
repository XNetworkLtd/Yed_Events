About Us

Bringing Laughter to Every Occasion

We are a UK-based comedy event company dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences through world-class stand-up performances. From corporate functions and charity galas to private parties and special celebrations, we connect audiences with exceptional comedic talent.

Our team handles every aspect of the show, from artist booking and event planning to on-site coordination, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience. We believe that great comedy brings people together and turns every gathering into a cherished memory.

Our Vision

To become the UK’s most trusted name in live comedy, creating unforgettable moments of laughter for audiences everywhere.

Our Mission

To connect exceptional comedians with events of every size, delivering seamless, professional, and joy-filled experiences.

Our Story

Founded from a passion for stand-up comedy, we set out to make world-class live entertainment accessible for every celebration.

Meet The Founder

Dhruv Dev

Dhruv Dev is an MSc Sports Events Management student with a passion for creating memorable live experiences. His academic background, combined with hands-on event involvement, has shaped his commitment to delivering exceptional comedy and entertainment events.

As a volunteer at the opening ceremony of LEEDS 2023 Year of Culture at Headingley Stadium, Dhruv helped welcome guests, coordinate the audience light show, and support the smooth running of the event. Witnessing the spectacular performances, fireworks, and drone displays reinforced his belief that great events have the power to bring people together and create lasting memories.
